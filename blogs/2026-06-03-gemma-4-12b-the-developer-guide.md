Google announces Gemma 4 12B, a multimodal model featuring an encoder-free architecture that processes vision and audio directly within the LLM backbone. The model runs locally on consumer hardware and includes new macOS desktop applications for developers to build local AI agents with improved latency and unified fine-tuning capabilities.
