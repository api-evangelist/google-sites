Google's new Gemma 4 12B model brings AI capabilities directly to laptops through the Google AI Edge stack. The release includes three key applications: Google AI Edge Gallery for local coding tasks, Google AI Edge Eloquent for voice-based text editing, and LiteRT-LM CLI with a new serve command for creating fully-local agentic tools.
